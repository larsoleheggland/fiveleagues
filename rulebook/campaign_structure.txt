# Campaign Structure

## Starting the Campaign

### Step 1: Set Up the Region
Name the region.

### Step 2: Establish Settlements
Roll D100 on Settlement Population Table:
01-10: 0H, 1V, 1T | 11-30: 1H, 1V, 1T | 31-45: 2H, 0V, 1T | 46-60: 0H, 2V, 1T | 61-75: 1H, 2V, 1T | 76-90: 2H, 1V, 1T | 91-100: 2H, 2V, 1T

Roll Settlement Type Table for each (D100, -10 hamlet, +10 town):
10 or lower: Scattered Farming Community (Upkeep max 2GM)
11-20: Military Outpost (+1 Recruit roll)
21-30: Nomadic Camp (can Hunting Expedition here)
31-40: Monastery (+1 Study roll)
41-55: Manor (blacksmith repairs 2 items)
56-80: Market Town (first visit +1 AP)
81+: Trading Hub (pay 1GM for extra Rare Goods roll/turn)

Quick Play: First=Town(Market), Second=Hamlet(Monastery), Third=Hamlet(Manor), Fourth=Village(Market)

### Step 3: Create Your Map
Mark settlements, fill 4-5 place names. Map Areas (not fixed size). Wilderness = unexplored. Locations = interactable points.
- Hidden Locations: not on map until found via Adventure Milestone
- Unexplored Locations: known but not investigated (cap at 4 optional)
- Moving: Travel roll required between Map Areas. Within same Map Area = no Travel needed.

### Step 4: Determine Threats
Foes Within (select 2): Ruin Within, Whispers from Beyond, Gnawling Horde, Ice-heart Court
Foes Without (select 1, or roll D100): 01-25 Duskling, 26-50 Oldest Kin, 51-75 Curse of War, 76-100 Faceless Kingdom

### Step 5: Establish Threat Levels
Primary enemy = Threat Level 6. Other two = Threat Level 5. Goal: reduce all to 0.

### Step 6: Place Additional Points of Interest
1 Delve (away from settlements), 1 Unexplored Location, 1 Camp per Threat (in Wilderness).

### Step 7: Note Down Hideouts
Each Threat has 1 Hidden Hideout (not on map until discovered).

### Step 8: Select Start Point
Edge of map. Travel roll needed to reach first location.

## The Campaign Structure
4 stages per turn: Preparation → Adventuring → Encounter → Resolution

## Adventure Points
Track for warband. Region-specific (reset when leaving).
Earning: Victorious Threat/Raid=D6, Patrol=1, Aberration=1 each, Unique Foe=2, Lt/Captain=1, Contract=1.
Plus various event bonuses.

## Adventure Milestones
Spend 1-6 AP from pool. Roll D6: if ≤ points spent = achieved. Points lost either way.
Uses: Locate Hidden Location, Gather Money, Upgrade settlement, Reduce Threat, Promote Follower.
Each type once per turn, multiple types OK.

## Preparation Stage
In Town (same Map Area as settlement) or In Camp (no settlement or Wilderness).
First turn: skip Preparation, go to Adventuring for Travel roll.

### Step 1: Local Events
Roll D100 on Town Events or Camp Events table.

### Step 2: Hard Times
- Upkeep Costs: 1-6 warband=D3, 7-8=D3+1, 9-10=D6, 11+=D6+1
  If can't pay: random member becomes Disgruntled (or loses Loyal, or Disgruntled leaves)
- Healing: If paid upkeep, all recovering reduce by 1 turn. If not paid, only 1 character reduces. Tonics cut 1 additional turn.

### Step 3: Campaign Activities (choose 2)
Settlement only: Blacksmith, Gamble, Hard Work, Help Town Guard, Meet Locals, Recruit, Visit Healer
Camp only: Forage Herbs, Hunting Expedition, Live off Land, Mystic Expedition, Scout
Either: Pray, Study, Train

(Each activity's details are extensive - see the full rulebook for specific mechanics)

### Step 4: Trade (Settlement only)
Buy from Common Goods (1 of each per turn). Village=1 Rare Goods roll, Town=2. Sell items for 1GM (enchanted in Town = D6 GM). Sell valuables at listed value. Can attempt 2 Adventure Milestones (Gather Money, Invest in Community).

### Step 5: Research
Roll for connections (D100). Discover Hidden Locations via Adventure Milestone.

### Step 6: Decide Your Adventure
Options: Explore location, Battle Enemy Threat, Ride Patrol, Carry out Contract, Rest Up, Quest (if applicable).

### Step 7: Outfit for Adventure
Backpack holds 8 items. Anything not packed = Stashed. Weapons/armor on characters don't count.

## Two Battles in One Turn
If battle while traveling:
- Complete Resolution normally
- May use healing items
- Select 1 injured character, reduce recovery by 1 turn
- Must complete all encounters before next turn
- Enemy numbers -1 for second battle
- Ignore suggested 3rd battle while traveling
