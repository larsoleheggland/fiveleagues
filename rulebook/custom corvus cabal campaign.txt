================================================================================
THE CROWS OF ASHWICK — Custom Mini-Campaign
================================================================================

A focused campaign for Five Leagues from the Borderlands (approx. 6 sessions).
Uses standard rules with the modifications listed below.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONCEPT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A single enemy faction (The Ruin Within) at Threat Level 2. The crow-masked
bandits have taken hold of the Ashlands, choking trade and terrorizing the
settlements. The warband's goal: find and destroy their leader's hideout.

Campaign ends when:
- Threat Level is reduced to 0 AND the Hideout is destroyed.
- Or the warband is wiped out / players decide to move on.

Threat Level 2 means two successful reductions (each requiring a victorious
battle + Adventure Milestone) plus destroying the hidden Hideout. This leaves
room for contracts, patrols, delves, and exploration between threat battles.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
REGION: THE ASHLANDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Historically a burned-down section of forest that became a clearing. A river
runs through it. Some sections of dead wood remain, but the area is mostly
open land where people have settled. The fire happened long ago — no ash
remains.

Settlements:
- Ashwick     — village, Market Town (+1 AP on first visit), 1 Rare Goods roll
- Woodborough — town, Trading Hub (2 Rare Goods rolls, pay 1GM for a third)
- Pine Farms  — hamlet, Scattered Farming Community (max 2GM upkeep)

Wilderness:
- The Dead Woods — across the river from the settlements. Contains the crow
  camp and an unexplored location.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MODIFICATIONS TO STANDARD RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Single threat faction only (The Ruin Within at TL2 instead of three factions
   at TL5-6). No other threats on the map.

2. Turn 1 travel result is scripted as an Obstacle -> Toll Post manned by the
   crows (see Session 1 Plan below).

3. First fight drops the Defensive Battle +1 enemy modifier. Can be added back
   if the numbers roll was kind.

4. Ashwick has a custom contract table replacing the standard Connections roll
   (see below). All contracts tie to the crow storyline.

5. Everything else runs by the book.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CUSTOM CONTRACT TABLE — ASHWICK TOWN COUNCIL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Available when arriving in Ashwick. Roll D6. Reward: D6 Gold Marks.
Use the standard contract rules from the book for the chosen type.

D6  Type                Story Hook
--  ------------------  ---------------------------------------------------
1   Chase Off Group     The crows have a forward camp near Pine Farms,
                        raiding root cellars at night.
                        Time limit: 3 turns.

2   Deliver Goods       A medicine shipment for Woodborough — no trader
                        will risk the road.
                        Time limit: 4 turns.

3   Aid Community       Pine Farms is losing livestock. Farmers need a
                        visible armed presence.
                        Time limit: 5 turns.

4   Rescue Individual   A woodcutter went into the Dead Woods three days
                        ago. The crows probably have him.
                        Time limit: 2 turns to visit.

5   Escort Individual   A merchant needs safe passage to Woodborough —
                        too many crow sightings on the road.
                        Time limit: 3 turns to pick up.

6   Gather Information  The council suspects someone in town is fencing
                        stolen goods for the crows. They need proof.
                        Time limit: 2 turns.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SESSION 1 PLAN — ARRIVAL IN THE ASHLANDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

TURN 1 — Travel to Ashwick
  First turn rule: skip all Preparation, go straight to Adventuring Stage.

  Scripted result: Obstacle -> Toll Post, manned by the crows.

  Narrative:
    Crow-masked figures lounge on the side of the road. Tattered black cloaks,
    jagged blades resting on shoulders. One steps forward, head cocked birdlike,
    and holds up two fingers. "Toll to enter the Ashlands. Two gold. The
    Mistress demands her due."

  The players choose:
  - Pay 2 GM — arrive at Ashwick broke (1 GM left) but unharmed.
  - 9+ [Speech] test (Sigrid has Speech) — bluff past. Failure: they attack.
  - Fight — Defensive Battle vs Ruin Within (escalating: Desperate Mob).

  The Desperate Mob:
  - Numbers: Roll 2D6 keep highest, then +3 (range 4-9).
    Defensive Battle normally adds +1 more but we are dropping it.
    If the roll was kind, add it back.
  - Stats: Speed 4"/+2" | CS +0 | Dmg +0 | Tough 3 | Armor 0 | 2 slingers
  - Break point: No normal Morale dice. They flee when total casualties
    exceed half their starting number. At 7 enemies: breaks after 4 kills.
    At 8: after 5.

  After the battle (if fought):
  - Resolve normally (injuries, XP, loot).
  - This is a travel encounter, NOT a Threat battle — no threat reduction,
    no threat AP.

TURN 2 — Ashwick (full Preparation turn)
  Run Steps 1-7 from the book.
  - Ashwick is a village (Market Town): first visit +1 AP, 1 Rare Goods roll.
  - A contract is available from the Town Council. Roll D6 on the custom
    contract table above (skip or replace the Connections roll).
  - Pick an adventure for this turn: Battle Threat, Ride Patrol, Carry out
    Contract, or Explore.

Session goal: Arrive in the Ashlands, encounter the crows, complete a full
preparation turn in Ashwick, pick up a contract.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CAMPAIGN ARC (ROUGH OUTLINE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sessions 1-2:  Arrive, establish in Ashwick, first encounters with the crows.
               Pick up contracts, do patrols, earn AP.

Sessions 3-4:  Raid the crow camp (Scenario C). Earn Vital Information.
               Explore the unexplored location or a delve. Start spending AP
               on threat reductions and locating the hidden Hideout.

Sessions 5-6:  Hideout Raid (Scenario D) — the climactic battle. Destroy the
               hideout and reduce the threat to 0. Wrap up any loose contracts.

This is a rough guide, not a script. Let the dice and player choices shape the
actual pace.
