# Resolution & Advancement

## The Resolution Stage
Skip if no battle this turn. Steps in order:

## Step 1: Collect Adventure Points
Activity | AP Gained
Victorious Enemy Threat or Raid | D6
Victorious Ride Patrol | 1
Defeat an Aberration | 1 per Aberration
Defeat a Unique Foe | 2
Defeat a Lieutenant or Captain | 1
Finish a Contract | 1

### Reduce Threats
If fought Threat and Achieved Objective, attempt Adventure Milestone. If achieved, Threat -1. At 0 with all Hideouts destroyed = Threat eliminated.

## Step 2: Check for Injuries / Flight in the Dark
Characters removed as casualties roll D100 on Injury Table. Retreating may require Flight in the Dark roll.

### Injury Table (Heroes) D100
01-20: Dead (Sorry, +1 SP)
21-30: Serious (Roll 2D6, pick highest)
31-40: Moderate (D6)
41-60: Light (D3)
61-75: Item damage (-)
76-100: Knocked out (0)

### Injury Table (Followers) D100
01-30: Dead (Sorry)
31-60: Moderate (D6)
61-75: Item damage (-)
76-100: Knocked out (0)

### Flight in the Dark Table (Heroes) D100
01-05: Slain while fleeing (Sorry)
06-20: Injured while fleeing (D6)
21-35: Lost equipment (-)
36-50: Item damage (-)
51-100: Escaped unscathed (-)

### Flight in the Dark Table (Followers) D100
01-10: Slain while fleeing (Sorry)
11-30: Injured while fleeing (D6)
31-45: Lost equipment (-)
46-60: Item damage (-)
61-100: Escaped unscathed (-)

### Injury Results
- Dead: Character perishes. Items recovered, random weapon/armor Damaged. +1 SP if Hero.
- Slain while fleeing: Lost with all carried items.
- Serious/Moderate/Light/Injured while fleeing: Roll indicated die for recovery turns.
- Lost equipment: Random weapon/shield/helmet lost.
- Item damage: Random weapon/armor becomes Damaged.
- Knocked out/Escaped unscathed: No lasting effects.

### Lucky Characters: Spend Luck point to ignore result (permanent).
### Silvertree Leaf: Reroll Injury Table (not Flight in Dark). New result counts. If would have died, +D3 recovery turns.
### Helmet: Double roll (11,22,33...) = knocked out, helmet Damaged.

## Step 3: Check for Advancement

### Experience Points
Survived encounter: +1 XP (all participants)
Enemy Leader slain: +1 XP (Hero delivering killing blow)
Heroic slayer: XP = Aberration Reward Rating (distributed)
Held Field* or Achieved Objective*: +1 XP (all Heroes on table) *not both

### Advancement Table (XP thresholds)
4=1st, 9=2nd, 15=3rd, 22=4th, 30=5th (become Loyal), 39=6th, 50=7th, 65=8th (Enemy Plans roll), 80=9th, 95=10th (Quest obtained), 110=11th, 125=12th (+1 Recruit), 140=13th, then +15 per advancement

### Advancement Subtable (non-Mystic) D100
01-30: Speed increase or skill
31-50: Agility increase or Toughness increase
51-85: Combat Skill increase
86-100: Luck increase or Will increase

### Advancement Subtable (Mystic) D100
01-20: Skill
21-40: Agility increase or Speed increase
41-55: Casting increase or Will increase
56-80: Casting increase
81-100: Spell improvement

### Advancement Details
Speed: 1st 6"/+3, 2nd 6"/+4, 3rd 7"/+4
Agility: 1st→2, 2nd→3, 3rd→4
Toughness: 1st→4, 2nd→5, 3rd→6
Combat Skill: 1st→+1, 2nd→+2, 3rd→+3 (melee or ranged only)
Casting: 1st→+1, 2nd→+2, 3rd→+3
Luck: each increase = 1 point (can be lost)
Will: 1st→1, 2nd→2 (Human only), 3rd→3 (Human only)
Spell improvement: 1st new random spell, 2nd known spell -2 Incantation, 3rd start with 4 Strands, 4th new random spell
If maxed one option, must pick other. If both maxed, skill of choice.

### Follower Advancement (Flash of Insight) D100
01-55: Nothing
56-70: Skill if character has none
71-85: Skill of choice
86-95: Becomes Hero (no other changes)
96-100: Becomes Hero + one Advancement roll

Promoted Heroes become Loyal. Can also promote via Adventure Milestone.

## Step 4: Roll for Loot
If Achieved Objective: 1 Loot Table roll. Aberration: rolls = Reward Rating.
Some scenarios/enemies grant extra rolls.

## Step 5: Roll for Unusual Finds
If Held Field: D100 on Unusual Finds Table (by scenario type).
Results include: nothing, battered weapons, coins, victim remains, captive, excellent weapon, valuable goods, tracks, strange sack, personal item, key individual slain, lead to location, grateful volunteer, evidence of plot.

## Step 6: Settle In
Same Map Area as settlement: begin next turn In Town or In Camp. Elsewhere: In Camp.

## Step 7: News Travels
Roll D100 on News Table. Various results including unexplored locations, monster sightings, Threat changes, quests, etc.

## Enemy Plans (D100)
Triggered at various points. If all Threats eliminated, ignore.
Cannot be affected by Story Points.
Results range from gathering weapons (01-07) to dark rituals (96-100).
Include all entries with full effects.

## Victory and Defeat

### Clearing a Threat
Reduced to 0 + all Hideouts destroyed. Rewards: 1 Loot roll, +1 SP.

### Clearing the Region
All Threats cleared = campaign won. Additional: +1D6 SP, +2D6 GM.

### Player Victory
Can declare victory at any point for any reason.

### Player Defeat
All warband dead, or all settlements destroyed.

## The Next Campaign
Step 1: Carry over GM, equipment, SP. AP reset to 0.
Step 2: Dead Avatar: 2D6, 9+ = returns. Remove Avatar status.
Step 3: Heroes: D6 each (-1 if Disgruntled). 0-2=retire, 3-6=available. Loyal don't roll.
Step 4: Followers: D6 each (-1 Disgruntled). 0-3=retire, 4-6=available. Loyal don't roll.
Step 5: Friends: D6 each. 1-3=gone, 4-6=remain.
Step 6: Under-strength: fill with past settled characters or new Followers.
Step 7: Appoint new Avatar.
Step 8: Create new region.
