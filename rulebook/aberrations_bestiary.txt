# Aberrations & Bestiary

## What Are Aberrations
Catch-all for creatures so powerful they transcend normal rules. Use Monster Points to track damage. Have restrictions on Actions and movement.

## Monster Points (MP)
When striking an Aberration, Armor works normally, but Overcome Toughness modified:
- Natural 1 or below Toughness = no effect
- Equal to Toughness = -1 MP
- Above Toughness: -1 MP if currently 3 or less; -2 MP if currently 4+
Destroyed at 0 MP. Ignore direct Wound effects. Cannot be Stunned.

## Engaging in Combat
- Cannot receive Combat Bonus from allies (but opponents can)
- All Aberrations may Counter Attack
- Profile lists melee damage values

## Aberration Actions
- Can't Dash but move as fast as possible by most direct route toward closest target
- Push aside Human-sized allies while moving
- Completely unaffected by Morale

## Slayer's Rewards
Reward Rating in profile. Multiple Aberrations: highest Rating +1 per additional.
- Gold Marks = Reward Rating
- Bonus XP = Reward Rating (distribute evenly among surviving Heroes)
- Loot rolls = Reward Rating
Per Aberration slain: +1 Story Point, +1 Adventure Point

## Unique Creatures
Each entry encountered only once per campaign as random monster. If re-rolled, use first unencountered. Events can cause repeats. List resets at campaign end.

## Aberration Traits
- Area Attack: After move (no melee), ranged at closest warband in range. Hit on 4+. Within 2" of target hit on 5+. Cover/protections don't apply. Can hit allies.
- Charge: Combat Bonus in first melee exchange if moved into melee.
- Cruel Wounds: Casualty adds 1 turn to Injury Table recovery.
- Difficult to Kill: Each Tracking Phase if didn't fight melee, D6 5-6 = regain 1 MP.
- Ethereal: Never lose more than 1 MP to a single strike.
- Fell Touch: Blows always penetrate non-enchanted Armor.
- Lash: When losing MP, pushes all melee opponents 3" back, ends melee immediately.
- Knock Back: Melee strike flings character D6" back. Collision with character = stop. Ends melee.
- Magic Resistant: Unaffected by spells; enchanted weapons provide no advantage. Character-affecting effects still apply.
- Ranged Attack: If not in melee after move, ranged attack like archer. Own damage score. No ammo limit.
- Size: Ranged attacks get +1 to Hit.
- Terror: Follower losing melee exchange flees full move away, no Actions next round. If engaged, fights normally.
- Venom: Natural 6 on combat roll knocks out target, bypassing Armor.
- Wings: Ignore terrain penalties, cross obstacles/Impassable. Land at end of move.
- Undying: Counts as Undead for items/spells.

## Creating New Monsters
Use existing profile and rename, swap traits, or create from scratch.
Reward Rating formula: (+1 per MP, +1 per Combat Skill point, +1 per highest melee damage bonus, +1 per trait except Size) ÷ 4 rounded up.
