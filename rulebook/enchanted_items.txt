# Enchanted Items

## General Rules
- Cannot be Damaged (except consumed potions)
- Can be sold for D6 GM in town only
- Usable by any character (Hero or Follower)

## Abbreviations
(S) Single-use | (R) Ranged weapon | (M) Melee weapon | (DB) During battle only | (OC) Out of combat or during battle | (W) Worn item | (B) Backpack item | (C) Campaign item

## Enchanted Items Table (D100)
01-15: Weapon | 16-35: Potion | 36-50: Clothing | 51-65: Armor | 66-80: Jewelry | 81-95: Magical Apparatus | 96-100: Unique Items

---

## Enchanted Weapons
Any character can use. Hit +0/+0 unless noted. Subject to ammo rules. Physical form doesn't matter - only grants described benefits, never normal weapon effects.

### Enchanted Weapon Subtable (D100)
01-07: Battering Storm - Wielder loses melee on draw. All Hits +1/+2.
08-15: Champion's Will - Enemies can't gain Combat Bonus from allies vs wielder.
16-23: Finding Knife - Melee normally. Can throw (Range 9", -1/+0), returns.
24-31: Fury Biter - Ignore Stun in melee. If would roll Flight in Dark, roll Injury Table instead.
32-36: Fury of Life - Vs Undead: gain Parry and Counter Attack.
37-43: Gleaming Friend - Draw = won by wielder instead.
44-50: Hate Strike - When losing melee exchange, inflict Hit on enemy simultaneously.
51-58: Heart Seeker - Natural 6 as Attacker in melee = auto bypass Armor.
59-65: Mountain's Bane - Failed Armor penetration = target Stunned.
66-71: Penetrating Dart - Range 24". Kill = auto Hit another target within 4" (max 1 extra).
72-78: Ruin Edge - Reduce Aberration MP by 1 additional when reducing MP.
79-83: Shadow Flight - Range 24". Target always counted as in the open. Shoot through forest/bushes. Can't shoot through solid obstacles.
84-90: Soul Render - All Hits +1/+1.
91-95: Swift Eagle - Unlimited range. +1 to Hit when shooting.
96-100: Venom Fang - Wounding opponent in melee = slain (unless Fey or Undead).

### Enchanted Armor Subtable (D100)

13-22: Bright Maille - Armor 3. +1 melee vs Undead, -1 vs Fey.
23-30: Curative Harness - Armor 2. Tracking Phase: if didn't fight melee, heal Wound.
31-43: Harness of Vengeance - Armor 2. If Wounded/casualty from melee, striker suffers same fate.
44-50: Impervious Plate - Armor 3. No Overcome Armor bonuses from any source.
51-62: Maille of Shadows - Armor 2. Ranged troops ignore wearer unless within 9". Making ranged attack nullifies for rest of battle.
63-69: Plate of Never-Ending Dreams - Armor 3. Can't be Stunned, enemies no Combat Bonus in melee.
70-80: Radiant Chest-plate - Armor 2. Ranged attacks vs wearer or within 2" Hit only on natural 6.
81-92: Stout Defender - Armor 3. Can Parry in melee.
93-100: Traitor's Harness - No Armor value. When struck D6: on 6 = casualty (becomes non-magic partial armor). On 1-5 = deflected. Armor bonuses don't apply, but Armor-ignoring effects work normally.

14-25: Ferret's Drink - One battle: +3"/+0" Speed, no terrain reductions. No Flight in Dark roll.
26-33: Fiery Wine - One battle: ranged 9" fire attack, unlimited ammo. +1/+0 (+1/+1 vs Undead).
34-45: Potion of Valley Mist - Reduce injury recovery by 3 turns.
46-51: Serpent Water - Permanently immune to venom/Poison trait.
52-58: Shadowy Ale - Incorporeal for current + next 2 rounds. Can't attack/interact, can't be attacked. No terrain penalties, move through solid. No Flight in Dark if leaves table.
59-74: Spring Vial - Heal any Injury Table result including death. Companion can administer. May roll all injuries first.
75-83: Wasp Mead - One battle: all melee/ranged attacks have Poison (natural 6 = auto knockout unless Armor saves).
84-89: Winter Flask - Roll on Injury Table immediately. If survive: +2D6 XP. Hero only.
90-100: Wolf Potion - One battle: all melee Hits +1/+1 (replaces normal weapon).

## Magical Apparatus
Used by Mystics for spellcasting.

### Magical Apparatus Subtable (D100 - exact ranges from rulebook)
- Arcane Notes: Read In Camp/Town. Select known spell, reduce Incantation by -1 permanently.
- Ensorcelled Scroll: Random spell from table. Cast by Mystic as if known (normal roll). If already known: +2 to Casting roll.
- Congealed Strands: D3+1 Strands found. Use in place of normal, don't expire.
- Mystic Crystal: Use Action. Cast known spell without Strand or Casting roll.
- Runestone: +1 to all Casting rolls.
- Spellbook Tome: Read In Camp/Town. Random spell learned. If known: +3 XP.

## Unique Items (C, S)
Campaign items. Single-use. OC items usable anytime in campaign turn or as Use Action in battle.

### Magical Unique Items Subtable (D100)
01-07: Bone Knife (S,C,DB) - Before battle: D6 characters start Wounded. Per Wound = 1 Skeleton minion (Speed 4"/0", CS +0, T4, Armor 1, can't Dash, Tough, Fearless, immune to Poison/Terrifying). Act in Slow Actions Phase.
08-19: Dragon's Spite (S,C,DB) - Ranged attack at target point within 6". All within 2" struck (no Hit roll). +1/+1 Hit. Survivors flee D6" away. Undead: +1/+2, don't flee.
20-30: Orb of Winter Fog (S,C,DB) - Thick fog. Retreat any characters without Flight in Dark. If battle continues: 6" visibility rest of battle.
31-43: Seeds of Spring (S,C,OC) - Start of turn: Camp event = "Nothing happens" option. Injured reduce by 1 turn. No Upkeep.
44-50: Soul Sphere (S,C,OC) - Revive any character slain this campaign turn.
51-62: Spider Whispers (S,C,DB) - Summon Hero (Agility 1, Speed 4"/+3", CS +1, T4, bastard sword, partial armor). Place 9"+ from all figures. Fights for battle then leaves.
63-69: Talisman of Oldest Mother (S,C,DB) - Roll on Aberration Table. Arrives at nearest battlefield edge in Tracking Phase. Acts in Slow Actions Phase, player-controlled.
70-80: Teeth of the Stalker (S,C,DB) - Immediately move 9" (ignore rough terrain). Can enter melee. If so, roll twice pick best in first exchange.
81-88: Thorn of Ice (S,C,DB) - Select point within 9" and LoS. Any Wounded character within 3" = casualty.
89-100: Tiny Offering (S,C,DB) - Before battle: every Follower gets +1 CS, +1 Toughness for battle.
